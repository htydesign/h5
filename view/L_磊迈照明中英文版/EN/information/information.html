Company Profile

Lei technology was founded in 2004, is a collection of LED outdoor landscape lighting lamps and lanterns and light tube control management system of research and development, production, sales in one of the state-level high-tech enterprises.Lei step after several years of science and technology deep and wide range of products and market investigation and study, with the help of the pearl river delta local ample technical talent pool and mature production process, quickly established a including LED research and development, testing, manufacturing and quality control, complete production system, and set up a research and development and production base in shenzhen baoan... Lei mai technology has a high-quality professional technical team... , as well as a number of large and medium-sized cities throughout domestic marketing and service network, with excellent quality and reliable performance of the lamps and lanterns, has completed hundreds of domestic LED lighting project, which involved in the background the Olympics, the world expo in Shanghai, xi 'an the park such as national key lighting project;At the same time, products are exported to Europe, America, Middle East, South America, southeast Asia and other countries and regions, by the vast number of customer recognition and enjoy good reputation both at home and abroad. Lei mai technology has always been adhering to the "integrity of Shared, rigorous pragmatic" business philosophy, with the domestic and international lighting engineering company, design company, to make joint efforts to create a better light environment. Lei technology of mission: to create value for customers, for employees to create wealth. Lei mai technology vision: to create a bright light environment.
